Family Office · Succession

Succession and Will Planning

India is witnessing a generational wealth transfer unlike any before. Without the right structures in place, that wealth does not pass. It fragments, disputes, and diminishes.

The Indian Context

A Wealth Transfer Crisis In Plain Sight

India's courts are overwhelmed with inheritance disputes. Around 85% of court cases related to inheritance are triggered by disputed or absent wills. The majority arise not from bad intentions, but from the absence of clear, legally sound documentation and properly structured succession plans.

The consequences go beyond legal fees and timelines. They fracture families, erode businesses, and undo decades of careful wealth accumulation in a matter of months. 3 in 4 HNI families have no formal succession plan at the time of a patriarch's passing, and 70% of family businesses do not survive the transition to the second generation without one.

The Challenge

Why Families Face Disputes

01
~80%
of Indians die intestate

Disputed Wills

Informal or improperly executed wills are challenged routinely in Indian courts. A will without correct witnesses, registration, or clarity on asset description can be rendered void, leaving distribution to courts.

02
₹78,000 Cr
unclaimed assets with IEPF

Undocumented Assets

Portfolios spanning mutual funds, equities, real estate, and overseas holdings often lack a centralised inventory. Heirs spend years locating assets, with a significant portion never recovered at all.

03
70%
of family businesses fail by 2nd gen

No Succession Plan

Family businesses represent a significant share of India's private wealth. Without documented succession arrangements, ownership transitions become contested, operationally damaging, and often terminal.

04
32M+
NRIs with cross-border exposure

Cross-Border Complexity

Families with NRI members, overseas accounts, or foreign property face conflicting inheritance laws across jurisdictions. Even valid Indian wills may fail to govern overseas assets effectively.

Our Planning Services

Will Drafting and Registration

Legally sound, precisely worded wills that clearly express your wishes across all asset classes. We ensure proper execution, witness protocols, and registration to minimise the risk of future challenge.

Trust Structuring

Private family trusts, discretionary trusts, and charitable trusts structured to protect assets, manage tax efficiency, and control distribution to beneficiaries over defined timelines and conditions.

Business Succession Planning

Structured transition plans for family-owned enterprises, defining leadership succession, ownership transfer, and continuity protocols to protect both the business and the family relationships built around it.

Cross-Border Estate Planning

Coordinated planning for families with NRI beneficiaries, overseas properties, or foreign investments. We navigate multiple jurisdictions to ensure consistent, enforceable arrangements.

Asset Inventory and Documentation

A comprehensive, centralised record of all assets, physical, financial, digital, and overseas, organised so that heirs can locate, claim, and manage the estate without confusion or delay.

Family Governance

Family constitutions, councils, and decision-making frameworks that establish shared values, define roles, and create the relational infrastructure needed to keep families aligned across generations.

How We Plan

Our Succession Planning Process

Discovery: A deep conversation to understand your family's full financial landscape, structure, and long-term intentions
Structure: Working with legal and tax specialists, we design the right combination of wills, trusts, and entity structures
Document: Every intention is reduced to clear, precise, legally enforceable documentation with full regulatory compliance
Review: Periodic reviews ensure your plan keeps pace as your family, assets, and circumstances evolve

Structured Solutions

Precision instruments for targeted market participation, designed to deliver defined outcomes within a clearly framed risk-and-return profile.

Overview

Investments Designed Around Your Outcome

Structured Solutions are built to deliver defined financial outcomes, offering capital protection, income generation, or participation in market movements within a precisely framed risk profile.

Whether your priority is preserving what you have, generating above-market income, or capturing a portion of market upside, these solutions can be tailored to match your specific view and risk tolerance.

Product Types

Capital Protected Products

Lock in a defined floor on your investment while maintaining exposure to potential market growth. Designed for investors who want downside certainty without stepping entirely away from market returns.

Yield Enhancement Products

Generate above-market returns by accepting a clearly defined level of downside risk. These strategies tend to perform well in stable or moderately rising market conditions and suit investors seeking enhanced income.

Participation Products

Capture a portion of market upside with full or partial principal at stake. The balance between participation rate and capital exposure is tailored to your market view and risk appetite.

Leverage Products

Amplify your market exposure with a magnified risk-return profile. Suited to experienced investors with a clear directional view who are willing to accept elevated risk for proportionally larger potential gains.

Why Structured Solutions

A Precise Tool In A Broader Portfolio

Transparent risk-return structure with defined terms at outset
Flexible to a wide range of market conditions and investment views
Available across multiple currencies, maturities, and underlying assets
Designed to complement and strengthen a broader investment portfolio

At PlusCapital, our investment approach is grounded in a set of core beliefs that apply consistently across all client portfolios.

These beliefs are not abstract principles. They are working convictions that shape how we construct portfolios, manage risk, and navigate uncertainty. They reflect decades of experience across market cycles and diverse client situations.

Core Beliefs

Our Investment Beliefs

Time Horizon Is Everything

The single most powerful variable in investing is not market timing or security selection - it is time. Investors who extend their horizon beyond short-term noise consistently capture returns that shorter-term thinking destroys.

Risk Must Be Understood, Not Avoided

Risk is not the enemy of returns - misunderstood risk is. We believe in clearly defining, measuring, and owning the risks in every portfolio, rather than building a false sense of safety through complexity or over-diversification.

Diversification Protects, But Not Blindly

True diversification means owning assets that behave differently under stress, not simply holding many of them. We build portfolios diversified across genuine risk factors, not just asset labels or geographies.

Conviction Without Flexibility Is Fragile

Holding convictions firmly is a virtue - but only when paired with the intellectual honesty to revise them when evidence demands it. Our process is disciplined and adaptive in equal measure, never dogmatic.

Our Process

Five Pillars of Our Investment Process

01

Secular Outlook

A periodic, disciplined assessment of the major long-term structural trends and themes shaping capital markets over the coming decade.

02

Strategic Allocation

Setting long-term asset allocation targets grounded in structural trends and forward-looking capital market assumptions, tailored to each client's objectives.

03

Tactical Allocation

Identifying near-term market drivers and adjusting positioning using a framework that distinguishes between four distinct capital market environments.

04

Portfolio Construction

Selecting instruments and building model portfolios in line with our construction principles: balancing conviction, liquidity, cost efficiency, and risk control.

05

Risk Management

Continuous, systematic monitoring of risk exposure at every level of the portfolio, throughout every stage of the investment process and across all market conditions.

Why It Matters

A Consistent Framework in an Inconsistent World

The same rigorous process applies to every client portfolio, regardless of size or complexity
Beliefs are stress-tested against real market cycles, not theoretical models alone
Long-term strategic clarity is maintained even when short-term market conditions create noise
Philosophy and process evolve with markets and evidence, never with emotion or trend-chasing

Market Intelligence

Sound investment decisions begin with sound information. Our research function delivers independent, timely analysis across global markets - giving clients the clarity to act with confidence.

Overview

Independent Analysis, Actionable Insight

Being well-informed is not optional in investing - it is the foundation. For clients navigating complex, fast-moving markets, access to unbiased, rigorous economic and financial research is one of the most valuable assets they can have.

Our research team combines deep macroeconomic expertise with granular market-level analysis, covering developed and emerging markets alike. Whether you follow broad economic trends or focus on specific asset classes, our intelligence gives your decisions a grounded, informed foundation.

Coverage Areas

What We Cover

Macroeconomics

Comprehensive coverage of developed markets, major emerging economies, and select frontier markets - tracking growth, inflation, monetary policy, and structural economic developments that shape the investment landscape.

Currencies

In-depth analysis of G10 and emerging market currencies, covering central bank policy, relative macro dynamics, and positioning trends to help clients manage cross-currency risk and opportunity effectively.

Fixed Income

Views on sovereign and corporate bond markets, yield curve dynamics, credit spreads, and duration positioning - essential context for any portfolio with a meaningful fixed income allocation.

Equities

Regional and sector-level equity views, including earnings trends, valuation frameworks, and factor analysis. Research is grounded in fundamentals but informed by market structure and technical dynamics.

Supply-demand analysis, geopolitical context, and price outlook across energy, metals, and agricultural commodities - relevant for both direct commodity exposure and broader portfolio inflation hedging.

Thematic and Technical Research

Dedicated research into structural megatrends linked to our Next Generation themes, alongside technical market analysis - combining the long view with near-term price behaviour to build a complete picture.
